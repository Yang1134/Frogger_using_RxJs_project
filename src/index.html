Frogger

Instruction:
This game is derived from the concept of the frogger game.
1. Get the green circle (frog) to the blue boxes at the other end.
2. Avoid the cars, which are the first 3 rows from the bottom.
3. Get some rest at the middle section.
4. Get on the logs to reach the other side, avoid the river to live. The river is blue.
5. Once you reach all 5 blue spots, score will be shown in the middle ground.
6. You only get 4 lives, cherish it.
7. To make it easy, the final row has no risk at all.
8. P.s. Reaching the blue box will take you back to the original position.
